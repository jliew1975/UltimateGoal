Second India Outreach

On Friday, August 18, 2017, a member from the FTC eBorg Robotics team returned to India to conduct a second outreach miles away in India. Our team introduced the students in a rosary school to robotics by giving an interactive presentation that teaches the students about our team, STEM and its importance, and robotics. Our team had a LEGO Mindstorms robot built and challenged the children in the shcool to attempt to program the robot, giving them a hands on experience with the programming aspect of robotics. As our team was teaching the students and demonstrating, you could see the excitement, eagerness, and curiousity light up in these students eyes as they grasped onto this new knowledge with the opportunity they were given. eBorg Robotics spent the day teaching the kids about the basics of robotics using a Lego EV3 kit and software. Our team was able to encourage more children into being a part of science, technology, engineering, and math, which are often subjects and careers that are overlooked. eBorg Robotics hopes to continue to serve and support local and global communities in a similar manner.

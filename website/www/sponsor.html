Our Sponsors

Thank you to all of our sponsors for helping contribute to our team's journey.

Our Services

The following companies have donated their services to the team and have contributed to the team's progress! Our services get:

  • Their company's name and logo (large) on our website
  • A description of their service on our website
  • Their company's name and logo (large) on our team t-shirt
  • Their company's name and logo (large) on our brochures and handouts
  • Their logo (large) on our robot
  • Their logo (large) on our banner
  • And a thank you email

Enrich Learning

Enrich Learning has been with our team since the very beginnning. They provided us a place to have meetings and sprout ideas whenever was needed and their learning center has become a place to gather to do some planning and work as a team. Without Enrich Learning, the team would not have had a consistent meeting place where the entire team was able to get together, communicate, plan, work, present, and make the impossible become reality!

Microsoft

Microsoft is one of the companies behind our hard-working team. By providing the team with the software they use to create important documents, presentations to companies, a shared space for their documents and work, and a form of easy communication through the team with email. Without Microsoft, the team would not be as efficient and productive as they are today!
